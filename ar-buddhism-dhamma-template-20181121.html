Overview

Perfect for offices that wishes to optimise workflow and enjoy quality colour prints

Designed for small and medium workgroups or enterprises, the imageRUNNER ADVANCE C3530i  is compact in size but big in features. Designed with intuitiveness, connectivity and quality colour prints, the iR-ADV C3530i series delivers optimal performance for your workplace.
